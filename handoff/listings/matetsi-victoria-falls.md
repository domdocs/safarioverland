## Verified facts (from public material)

- **Owner:** Gardiner family. Independently family-owned and operated.
- **Location:** Matetsi Private Game Reserve, Zimbabwe. On the Zambezi
  ~50 km west of Victoria Falls (geography from Dom's local knowledge).
- **Concession:** 136,000 acres (~55,000 hectares). Nine miles of
  private Zambezi river frontage.
- **Capacity:** 16 luxury suites + 2 family suites + 1 private villa
  (Matetsi River House) — call it 19 units, ~40–50 guests at peak.
- **Activities (confirmed):** Game drives, sunrise river cruises,
  sunset river cruises, Victoria Falls excursions, scenic flights via
  sister company Frontier Aviation (Pilatus PC-12 NGX), spa.
- **Wildlife:** Elephants, buffalo, lions, leopards.
- **Conservation programme:**
  - In-house **Amaganyane anti-poaching unit**, established mid-2021
  - Four anti-poaching vehicles donated to local units
  - 60+ km radio communications network, two dedicated networks
  - De-snaring and river net-collection operations
  - 16 solar-powered boreholes / waterholes
  - Annual controlled burns for habitat management
  - 14 waterbuck translocated from Hwange in June 2022
- **Community:**
  - 200+ staff. 99.5% Zimbabwean. ~60% from surrounding communities.
  - Junior guides trained on-site into tracker roles.
  - **GRACE Foundation** — education, healthcare, sustainable
    livelihoods partner.
  - **Pack for a Purpose** — guest-supplied community supplies.
  - **Greenline Africa Trust** — Northern Matabeleland community work.
- **Affiliations:** Virtuoso, ATTA, Mr & Mrs Smith, XO Private.
- **Sustainability Badge:** 2024.
